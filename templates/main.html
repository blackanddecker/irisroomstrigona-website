Our Story

Welcome to Iris Guesthouse

Established in 1988 along the main road from Igoumenitsa to Athens, Hotel Trigona has been part of countless guests' journeys. Today, more than 35 years later, that legacy continues through Iris Guesthouse.

What began as a humble, family-run hotel has evolved into a welcoming retreat for travelers from around the world. We combine traditional Greek hospitality with modern comfort.

The Place

A Peaceful Mountain Retreat

Nestled in the tranquil village of Trigona and surrounded by beautiful natural landscapes, our guesthouse is ideal for visitors seeking relaxation, outdoor adventure, or cultural exploration in the wider Kalambaka and Meteora region.

Every room is designed to feel like home — spacious, clean, and equipped with everything you need for a comfortable stay.

Perfect For

Who It's Ideal For

Adventure Seekers

Perfect for those who love hiking, climbing, and exploring the natural beauty of Meteora and the surrounding mountains. Our location offers direct access to trails, rock formations, and outdoor experiences that will take your breath away.

Couples

An ideal romantic escape with cozy rooms, serene views, and a peaceful atmosphere for rest and reconnection. Wake up to mountain mornings, enjoy coffee on your private balcony, and let the stillness of Trigona bring you closer together.

Families

A safe and family-friendly stay with spacious apartments, equipped kitchens, and comfortable outdoor spaces. Children can play freely in the garden while parents relax — everything you need for a worry-free family getaway.
