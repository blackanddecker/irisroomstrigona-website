Our Story

Established in 1988 along the main road from Igoumenitsa to Athens, Hotel Trigona has been a cherished part of countless guests' journeys, creating lasting memories over the decades. Now, 35 years later, it continues the legacy by offering the same warm, authentic hospitality through Iris Guesthouse. What began as a humble, family-run hotel has evolved into a beloved retreat for travelers worldwide. Combining traditional Greek charm with modern comforts, we strive to provide every visitor with a truly unforgettable experience.

Nestled in the tranquil village of Trigona, surrounded by breathtaking landscapes, we cater to those seeking relaxation, adventure, or cultural exploration. Our commitment is to make every guest feel at home while exploring the natural beauty of the region.
