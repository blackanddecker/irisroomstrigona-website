Welcome to Iris Guesthouse

A family place with a long history of welcoming travelers on their journey through central Greece.

Established in 1988 along the main road from Igoumenitsa to Athens, Hotel Trigona has been part of countless guests’ journeys, creating lasting memories over the decades. Today, more than 35 years later, that legacy continues through Iris Guesthouse.

What began as a humble, family-run hotel has evolved into a welcoming retreat for travelers from around the world. We combine traditional Greek hospitality with modern comfort, always with the goal of making every stay warm, peaceful, and memorable.

Nestled in the tranquil village of Trigona and surrounded by beautiful natural landscapes, our guesthouse is ideal for visitors seeking relaxation, outdoor adventure, or cultural exploration in the wider Kalambaka and Meteora region.
