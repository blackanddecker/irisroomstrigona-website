Hiking Meteora

Meteora offers a unique hiking experience through its awe-inspiring rock formations and serene monasteries perched high above the cliffs. Whether you’re an experienced hiker or a beginner, the trails around Meteora provide breathtaking views of the ancient monasteries, lush forests, and picturesque villages. Popular trails include routes from the village of Kastraki to the monasteries, offering panoramic views and peaceful moments amidst nature. Don't forget to bring your camera – every step offers a postcard-worthy scene!

Time from Guesthouse: 1 hour by car

Meteora Natural History Museum and Mushroom Museum

The Museum contains a rich collection of birds and mammals as well as a comprehensive mushroom museum, the first of its kind in Greece. The museum houses approximately 300 exhibits of animals and several dozens of the main species of mushrooms.
